Radiant Reverie

Interior Designing

Scope

Interior Designing

Style

We designed keeping in mind.

They had very basic needs but wanted to create a pleasing home. Dividing the kitchen, and the dining space was essential, as the use of dining was often in this house. We played in the false ceiling to create a lot of ambient & mood lighting.

The entry feature wall with the fairy light hanging right in the centre of attraction in the hall way played with the warmth and the positive intimate in the living room, and every element complements each other in the room. The metallic gold adds comfort, something the client was keen on having in their space.

The partition in coloured glass breaks the monotony compared to the contrast of the grey walls, and create a vibrant environment in the home.

There is a play of false ceiling and lighting to create moods and to satisfy the different objectives of the user.

Outside the Pooja, we've created a space for the yearly celebration of Ganpati Puja, which otherwise acts as a showcase area.

The Pooja space has been very beautifully incorporated in the with the Store room, to save space.

The bedrooms feature ambient lighting and modern design elements that create a peaceful atmosphere for rest and relaxation.
