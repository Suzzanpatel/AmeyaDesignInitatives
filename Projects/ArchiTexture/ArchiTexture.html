Stunning geometric light installation creating a focal point in the space, combining modern design with artistic expression. The suspended cubes create a dynamic visual rhythm while providing ambient lighting.

Clean, minimalist staircase design with integrated storage solutions, showcasing functionality with elegance. The white palette enhances the sense of space while maintaining visual interest through geometric forms.

The exterior was exposed brick. It had a very subtle language, and we took that forward, even in the interiors of the house, we see the use of colour in places in the rooms and in the kitchen. It gives a very homely and earthy vibe reflecting the characteristic of the user.

The design approach focused on creating a harmonious blend of textures and materials, bringing together modern aesthetics with functional living spaces. Each element was carefully chosen to reflect the client's personality while maintaining a cohesive design language throughout the space. The interplay of light and shadow adds depth to the interiors, while thoughtful space planning ensures optimal flow and functionality.

The ground floor room consisted of a guest room – this wardrobe being a part of it, designed with both aesthetics and functionality in mind. The clean lines and modern finishes create a sophisticated storage solution.
