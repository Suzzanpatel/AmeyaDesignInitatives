Project Overview

The home’s exterior showcases exposed brickwork, expressing a subtle yet warm architectural language. This earthy aesthetic is thoughtfully extended into the interiors through carefully chosen textures and color accents that resonate with the character of the residents. The design balances homeliness with sophistication, creating a seamless connection between the outside and inside. Functional zoning and personalized elements are integrated throughout to meet the family’s lifestyle and preferences.

Living Room

The ground floor living room serves as a formal yet inviting space, continuing the natural theme with a blend of jute textures and white exposed brick walls. This creates a tactile and visual harmony that reflects the home’s earthy exterior. A custom partition between the kitchen and dining area features a mix of open and closed cabinetry, ambient lighting, and a delicate glass art panel—defining spaces without interrupting flow or openness.

Rooms:

The ground floor guest room includes a thoughtfully designed wardrobe, combining aesthetics with practical storage. Anish’s bedroom draws inspiration from his love for music, with a false ceiling design subtly referencing musical elements. The back panel in the room serves as a quiet yet meaningful focal point. Additionally, spacious and functional dressing areas are incorporated within the bathrooms, tailored precisely to the users’ needs, demonstrating an intelligent approach to comfort and utility.
