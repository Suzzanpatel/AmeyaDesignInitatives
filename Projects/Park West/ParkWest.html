Park West

Style:

Quiet Luxury

Area:

1400 sq.ft

Project Overview

A warm, functional home designed to reflect the emotions and lifestyle of its residents. Each space is tailored to its purpose—whether it’s the inviting foyer, the joyful kitchen, or the serene bedrooms. Smart storage, layered lighting, and thoughtful material choices bring harmony, elegance, and character to every corner.


A Harmonious Welcome – Foyer & Living Room

Designed with deep sensitivity to the client’s emotions and lifestyle, the foyer and living area strike a balance between vibrancy and serenity. The intention was to seamlessly blend the formal and informal, catering to different moods of the home’s users. The foyer is a statement in simplicity and elegance, with thoughtful storage hacks integrated for utility without compromising on design. Soft lighting and warm materials set the tone, creating an inviting entrance.

The living space plays with light as a mood-setter. The lighting design transforms the atmosphere throughout the day—ensuring that whether it’s a lively gathering or a moment of stillness, the room adapts accordingly.

Soft Sophistication – Guest Bedroom

This compact bedroom posed a unique challenge, but the result is a serene space with an English-inspired palette of pale pink and oak wood. Despite the small size, every inch has been maximized through custom-designed wardrobes tailored to the client’s exact needs. The subtle tones bring warmth, and clever use of reflective surfaces creates a feeling of openness and depth—turning a tight layout into a welcoming haven.

A Joyful Corner – The Kitchen

As the heart of the home, the kitchen was designed to inspire happiness, energy, and wellness. The client’s brief emphasized vibrancy, and we responded with a bold bright yellow and soft grey palette. Despite being a compact space, the kitchen is now bursting with energy and purpose, thoughtfully designed to be both ergonomic and uplifting. From clever storage solutions to ambient lighting, every element ensures that cooking here becomes a joyful experience.

Warming Up the Ceiling – A Wooden Touch

An innovative wooden ceiling design adds warmth and character to the central spaces. Apart from its visual charm, it contributes to ambient lighting, which gently bathes the room in a soft glow. Details like lacquered glass add visual expanse and reflectivity, cleverly opening up smaller areas while enhancing the richness of the material palette.

A Grand Touch – The Master Bedroom

This master suite is a perfect example of grandeur in a compact setting. The combination of glass, warm hues, and wood textures creates a sophisticated and cozy atmosphere. A sliding wardrobe ensures smart space management, while ambient ceiling lights keep the space feeling bright and enchanting. A wooden frame behind the bed adds an elegant design element and helps define the space—visually expanding the room and bringing unity to the overall look.
