Interior Designing

We designed keeping in mind.

A space that is personal to all individuals, this living room is compact yet very serene. The modern LED lighting and comfortable seating create a welcoming atmosphere.

The use of fluted glass and modern materials enhances the space, creating an elegant and contemporary feel. The blend of textures and materials comes together beautifully.

The LED lighting design creates different moods and ambiance, perfectly complementing the modern aesthetic of the space.

A blend of modern and vibrant elements makes this living room both welcoming and sophisticated. The careful attention to lighting and material selection creates a perfect harmony.

They wanted to bring with them a few memories of their old home; the Hanuman frame in the foyer being one of those! We believe that a house can never truly be a home without the sentiments and emotions of the residents, so we made sure to incorporate it in our design.

The ambient lighting and carefully chosen decor elements create a warm and inviting atmosphere.

The kitchen design evolved from initial concepts to a bold and functional space that serves as a statement piece in the home.

The kitchen features a dedicated coffee station, perfect for starting the day with a fresh brew. The modern appliances and storage solutions make this space both functional and beautiful.

Every detail in the kitchen has been carefully considered, from the placement of appliances to the choice of materials and colors.

The master bedroom features a special macrame workspace, incorporating a custom-designed rod system that allows for creative flexibility.

Smart storage solutions and multifunctional furniture make the most of the available space while maintaining style.

The daughter’s room has a subtle look to it- the use of greys and blues, combined with the warm wooden laminate gives the room vastness and character. The stucco texture behind the TV blended perfectly with the design of the mirror and TV unit. There is a certain play of light in all the rooms including this one, making them look uniquely different in natural & artificial light.

The pastel color scheme and smart storage solutions create a harmonious blend of functionality and style.

The study-cum-pooja room was conceptualized keeping in mind their very specific requirements as they work from home and needed a space suitable for that. One of the main needs of the clients was that they did not want the wires of their various gadgets to be very visible. This was a maze that we had to very delicately handle so that the wires do not cause hindrance while being used and also provided a clutter-free look to the workspace.
