Elegantly Eclectic

Style:

Bohemian Luxe

Area:

820 sq.ft

Project Overview

This project celebrates thoughtful personalization—where aesthetic harmony meets individual stories. Each space has been designed to reflect the personality and lifestyle of its residents, with particular emphasis on emotional resonance, functional comfort, and subtle creativity. From hidden ducts and handcrafted details to cleverly concealed storage, this home is a narrative in warmth, ease, and intention.

Welcome with Warmth – The Foyer

The entrance sets the tone for this home—inviting, functional, and layered with meaning. A clever seating nook masks the external electrical duct while ensuring accessibility. Greenery takes center stage on custom plant shelves, a nod to the client’s love for nature. Sentimental value finds its place too, with the cherished Hanuman frame from their old home subtly incorporated into the foyer design.

The Comfort Core – Living & Lounge

This living space is a medley of comfort and detail. Responding to the clients’ desire for multiple seating options, we blended a lounge chair, recliner, and sofa with ergonomics and ease in mind. The rose gold-adorned TV panel becomes the room’s focal point, echoed by accents in the furniture.

The ceiling design adds vertical volume, while the pièce de résistance— a round art piece that cleverly conceals the electrical DB—demonstrates the fusion of creativity and utility. Subtle colour palettes provide calm, while filtered natural light and custom ambient lighting offer changing moods throughout the day.

The Daily Ritual – Kitchen & Coffee Nook

More than a utilitarian space, the kitchen is a zone of delight. Designed around the family’s cherished coffee ritual, it features a dedicated coffee corner, marrying style and functionality. Every inch is planned for convenience—from equipment storage to ergonomic movement—ensuring the heart of the home beats efficiently and beautifully.

Crafted for Creativity – The Macramé Workspace

A unique blend of utility and personal expression, this room houses the client’s macramé studio. A sleek folding table saves space while allowing creative work to flourish. The custom-built shelf includes a support rod tailored for macramé projects—subtle, smart, and highly functional.

The Master Bedroom

With a cane-laminate bed anchoring the space, this bedroom strikes a balance between warmth and texture. The materiality is soft yet rooted, aligning with the overall design intent. Every detail—from panelling to lighting—echoes the client’s aesthetics and brings a grounded elegance to the room.

Soft Tones, Strong Identity – Daughter’s Bedroom

This room carries a tranquil energy through its soft blues, greys, and warm wood tones. The stucco-textured wall behind the TV flows seamlessly with the integrated mirror unit, offering a cohesive, expansive feel. A subtle play of lighting ensures that the room transforms with the day—bright and open by morning, soft and inviting by night.

Mindful Multifunction – Study Meets Pooja Room

This dual-purpose space was carefully crafted for remote work and spiritual reflection. The layout optimizes workflow while preserving a meditative energy. One of the primary challenges—hiding the wiring from multiple devices—was addressed through smart routing and concealed channels. The result is a serene, clutter-free environment where focus and calm coexist.
