A harmonious blend of comfort and style, featuring a calming blue sofa set against a striking red brick accent wall.

A site converts into a home only after the residents start making memories there. The living room has seating, connected to opposite make them to feel very warm.

Traditional & contemporary, this room is the best of both worlds. Every detail adds to the look of the room - the choice of curtains, lights and the wooden finish in the false ceiling.

The soft pink tones create a peaceful and inviting atmosphere in this bedroom.

We love a pink room, and this one with it's pastel pink shade & dark wood is so sober & elegant.

Our client envisioned this room as a cozy room on a beautiful winter evening and we could not agree more!
