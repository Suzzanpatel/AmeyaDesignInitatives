A Serene Habitat

Style:

Contemporary Modern

Area:

870 sq.ft

Project Overview

A house becomes a home when it begins to gather memories. This project is a reflection of that journey — thoughtfully designed spaces that balance comfort, elegance, and personal character. Each room tells its own story, shaped by materiality, mood, and the lifestyle of its residents.

Living Room – The Heart of the Home:

A calming, connected, and vibrant space, the living room was designed to evoke warmth and serenity. A fine blend of traditional and contemporary aesthetics, the textures, color palette, false ceiling design, and furniture selection come together in perfect harmony. Key details — such as wooden ceiling finishes, carefully selected curtains, and ambient lighting — contribute to the overall sense of sophistication and liveliness. This room sets the mood for the entire home — grounded yet expressive.

Soft Elegance in Pink

This bedroom brings a refreshing sense of balance through its pastel pink tones and rich dark wood finishes. Every design element is deliberately composed — from the chamfered mirror edge to the fine detailing in the upholstery and bed design. Harmony was key: the furniture, materials, and colour scheme speak one design language, creating a cozy yet refined retreat. The result is a room that is elegant, calm, and visually seamless.

Master Bedroom

Designed to exude understated royalty and cozy comfort, the master bedroom is a study in warmth and sophistication. The curved, wave-inspired false ceiling softly envelops the room, while the warm lighting highlights the luxurious wooden finishes. Rich upholstery, elegant back paneling, and subtle gold T-patti accents imbue the space with regal charm. Every carefully considered detail contributes to a cohesive and inviting sanctuary.

Compact Bedroom – Smart Space, Strong Identity

This room demonstrates how smart design can elevate small spaces. Multi-functional elements such as a mirror integrated with a side table, and a study table that doubles as a TV unit, ensure efficient space usage without compromising aesthetics. Each element — colour, material, function — has been planned to contribute to a complete and coherent visual language. The room feels intimate, stylish, and practical — a true reflection of thoughtful residential design.
