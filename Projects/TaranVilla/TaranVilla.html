Construction Renovation and Interiors

Scope

Architectural and Interior

Design Philosophy

We designed keeping in mind:

A modern residential villa showcasing contemporary architectural design.

Transformation

Before
After

A home is a place of love, fun, happiness, functionality, and easy maintenance. An earthy look with the use of wood, exposed brick, and greenery was the direction. Re-decorating and restructuring was a task for the old building but it sailed through efficiently due to the team of workmen and labourers.

The living room is welcoming, soothing and bright with use of vibrant red and beige.

Smart design of otherwise bland staircase walls. Adds a pop of colour to the space.

The effect of natural light and yellow light in a space.

The master room has a lot of light from the west, hence the colour beige, cream and white was a challenge here.

The daughter's room with a bright and cheerful theme provides simple storage for cosmetics and clothes.

Kitchen and dining designed for easy cleaning, maintenance and efficient functional use with best use of space. The storage integrates with the kitchen, making it easily accessible.

A bathroom is a very important space to design for comfort, light and hygiene. These have been considered while designing the bathroom.
