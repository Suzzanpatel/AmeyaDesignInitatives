Cozy Corners

Style:

Youthful Monochrome

Area:

675 sq.ft

Project Overview

This residence beautifully merges spatial efficiency with aesthetic delight. Designed with a focus on comfort, light, and seamless functionality, every corner of this home tells a story—of cozy mornings, thoughtful detailing, and inviting atmospheres. From Santorini-inspired balconies to striking monochrome rooms, the design brings personal expression and spatial harmony together.

The Inviting Core – Living Room & Balcony

This subtle yet bright living room instantly draws you in with its open layout and an elegant nine-seater sofa that defines comfort. Overlooking a charming balcony, the space is both lively and serene. The TV unit and custom panel sit against a refined concrete-textured wall, offering the perfect counterpoint to the vivid upholstery.

A delicately designed false ceiling, paired with layered lighting, adds warmth and dimensionality to the room. The adjacent balcony, though compact, is thoughtfully designed for comfort and charm—with a swing, cozy seating, and lush greenery, it's the ideal winter-evening retreat. The palette of blue and white evokes a soft Mediterranean or Santorini-inspired vibe—airy, bright, and refreshingly tranquil.

Everyday Elegance – Kitchen, Breakfast & Dining Nook

Tucked beside the living room, the dining and breakfast space is compact yet highly efficient. It overlooks a large window, transforming the corner into a perfect spot for morning tea or evening conversations.

The kitchen, though modest in footprint, is carefully designed with ergonomic clarity and user comfort in mind. Deep blue cabinetry, accented with patterned tiles and warm wooden tones, brings texture and life to the space. A hanging pendant light above the breakfast table subtly ties the palette together, creating a visually delicate yet grounded ambience.

Bold Balance – Monochrome Room

This room is a study in balance—dark, sleek elements paired with thoughtful simplicity. Designed around a monochromatic palette, it features a black bed-back, vintage-style switches, and exposed piping that lend the space a distinctive industrial character.

The round mirror adds a sense of openness, expanding the visual perception of the room. Together, these elements create a minimal yet striking retreat—sophisticated without being stark.

Layered Tranquility – Master Bedroom

The master bedroom exudes calm with its soft lighting, earthy materials, and color-shifting walls that subtly change with the light. The ceiling hosts mood lighting directly above the bed, transforming the atmosphere from bright to intimate at the touch of a switch.
