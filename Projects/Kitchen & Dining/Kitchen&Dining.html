Kitchen & Dining

Style:

Contemporary Modern

Area:

6450 sq.ft

Project Overview

This kitchen and dining space was designed as a harmonious blend of functionality, emotion, and nature. The design embraces a dynamic play of light, colour, and material, resulting in a space that shifts in mood throughout the day — from calm to vibrant.

Design & Execution

The primary colour palette revolves around earthy tones, evoking warmth, calmness, and sophistication. Sparing yet intentional pops of vibrant red were introduced to bring energy and contrast to the dominant grey and white backdrop.

The lighting design plays a key role — nest-like pendant lamps add a natural, sculptural element while enhancing the warmth of the space. Live plants near the sink area create a refreshing connection with nature and introduce biophilic energy into the heart of the home

Functional Detailing

• The kitchen was designed with maximum storage and efficiency, ensuring ease of access to frequently used items and enabling faster, smoother workflow.
• Smart spatial planning ensures clutter-free surfaces while maintaining aesthetic appeal and ergonomic flow.

Dining & Emotional Anchoring

The dining area wall are dedicated to family memories, showcasing photographs and moments across generations. Set against a metallic stucco textured surface, the display becomes a visual anchor — adding personal warmth and narrative to the space.
