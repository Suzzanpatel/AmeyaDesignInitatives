Residence Reinvigorated

Style:

Glossy Contemporary

Area:

1200 sq.ft

Project Overview

This home is a beautiful union of comfort, sentiment, and elevated aesthetics. With each space designed around the user’s lifestyle and taste, the residence exudes warmth and thoughtfulness—from a serene pooja room reminiscent of a Garbhagriha to a bright, expansive living room and detail-rich bedrooms.

Welcome Home – Foyer with Character

A warm embrace right at the entrance, the foyer is adorned with rich walnut veneer, a graceful metal nameplate, and thoughtful design interventions that are both practical and elegant. The semi-circular jaali design and a stone sheet backdrop enhance the luxurious feel, while a wooden-framed open window invites natural light and adds dimension.

A harmonious blend of old and new, the existing shoe unit is seamlessly integrated into new paneling. Finishing touches like gold T-pattis, hanging planters, and a beautifully crafted safety door grill create a foyer that feels premium, personal, and inviting.

A Sanctum of Serenity – Pooja Space

This pooja room is nothing short of sacred design. Tucked behind an elegant fluted glass door, the space features a gypsum arch, copper-gold textured paint, and a pristine white PU unit. The divine metal idol takes center stage, accentuated by ambient lighting that gives the area a temple-like Garbhagriha feel. Every detail, from the scale of the arch to the glow of the lights, contributes to a peaceful, reverent atmosphere.

Minimal Elegance – Living & Dining Space

The living room echoes refinement through a white, grey, and wood theme that flows effortlessly into the dining and kitchen areas. Premium materials like acrylic alabaster panels, fluted glass, and PU-painted units are anchored by a warm wooden false ceiling and statement fans. The space feels expansive and harmonious. A unique built-in bench along the dining table adds character, while a custom white onyx tabletop with a sculptural wooden base brings movement to the area. A striking blue-grey-gold painting anchors the wall, injecting color and depth into the monochromatic setting.

Bright & Seamless – Kitchen with a Purpose

Function meets finesse in the kitchen, where thoughtful planning ensures maximum storage, flow, and natural light. By smartly concealing the refrigerator from the entrance, we opened up the hall and kitchen visually. The kitchen glows in artificial light and shines even more beautifully during the day, offering a warm, user-friendly space for culinary joy.

Refined & Restful – Master Bedroom

The master bedroom is a masterclass in blending functionality with design elegance. Floor-to-ceiling glass profile wardrobes open up the room visually, while the fluted charcoal paneling conceals the bathroom door in style. A fully upholstered bed sits under a ceiling specially crafted to hide structural elements while adding an accent design layer. The dune drizzle texture on the wall adds a luxe finish. Lighting has been carefully curated to create ambient warmth and layered moods, elevating the space’s premium appeal.

Sleek Sophistication – Greyscale Bedroom

Designed with a masculine touch and a strong sense of identity, this monotone grey bedroom is grounded in texture and contrast. With exposed concrete texture, a black patti detail, acrylic wardrobes, and PU-finished furniture in dark walnut tones, the room feels modern, minimal, and powerful.

A custom-designed study table adds functionality, while the lighting and material palette give the space a distinguished, designer edge.

Delicate Utility – Washroom Design

The bathrooms in this home are designed to be intuitive and elegant. One of our favorites features ambient lighting that highlights a beautiful wall tile, giving it a boho, evergreen charm. Every inch is optimized for user-friendliness, storage, and clutter-free usage, wrapped in soft aesthetics.

A Gentle Retreat – Parent’s Bedroom

This space is designed with care, considering the client’s mother’s comfort and preferences. Soft pastel tones, abundant lighting, and harmonious design details make it a truly welcoming retreat. The stucco-textured wall behind the upholstered bed, pastel green tones, acrylic panels, and gloss laminates come together to create a cohesive, expansive atmosphere. The wardrobe, mirror unit, and washroom door are seamlessly integrated, blending aesthetics with senior-friendly design.
