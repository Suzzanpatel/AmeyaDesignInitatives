Crafting DreamScapes

Style:

Earthy Natural

Area:

1210 sq.ft

Project Overview

A serene, earthy home designed for comfort and functionality. From a welcoming stone-clad foyer to a warm-toned living space with concrete textures and thoughtful detailing, every corner reflects calm and character. A vibrant teal kitchen forms the heart of the home, while cozy bedrooms and a user-friendly study balance elegance with purpose. Smart use of space, natural materials, and personalized touches make this home both practical and soulful

Foyer & Living Room

The entrance foyer sets the tone for the home — serene, functional, and welcoming. With stone cladding, storage, and a discreet shoe rack, the space is designed to blend aesthetics with everyday utility. Step into the white, wood, teal, and beige-toned living room, where a rich material palette brings warmth and calm. An arched concrete-textured wall acts as a design focal point, while the TV unit and tile partition add structure and visual depth. Elements like an aquarium and framed art introduce life, movement, and personality into the space.

Kitchen & Dining

In contrast to the earthy tones of the living area, the green and blue kitchen brings a refreshing pop of colour. Featuring glossy teal tiles, a granite-top breakfast table, and tile cladding, this space becomes the lively centre of the home. The kitchen is both functional and full of character — a space to gather, dine, and connect.

Bedroom & Study

The bedroom continues the warm, personal tone with wooden ceiling details, ivory shades, and carefully selected furnishings. The wood in the ceiling and the bed frame create cohesion, while a pouffe adds comfort and style.

Given the client's requirement to work from home, a user-centric study room was essential. The space is designed for functionality with ample work surfaces, ensuring productivity without compromising on aesthetic appeal.
