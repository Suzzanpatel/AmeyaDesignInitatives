Design Philosophy

We designed keeping in mind:

A contemporary living space featuring clean lines, neutral tones, and strategic lighting that creates a warm, inviting atmosphere.

This inviting foyer gives such calm and peaceful vibes. With stone cladding, sufficient storage and a shoe rack - all elements blend really well.

Entering in the white-wood teal-beige living room, with an architectural concrete texture adorning one wall & the TV unit & tile partition on another wall. This home is earthy and like a dream. An aquarium & photo frames on the wall- give the room life and movement.

The green & blue kitchen- a change from the living room- this room has a pop of colour. With tile-cladded and granite top breakfast table- this room is the centre of the whole house. The teal blue glossy tiles along with the aquarium & frames, give the room character & charm.

With elements of wood, photo frames, and ivory shades, gives the room a personal and warm vibe. The wood is the ceiling complements the wood in the bed room together.

The client wanted a work from home & immediate use of the study room- this room was made as user friendly as possible.

MDF wall paneling, with ample bed back, & false ceiling & pop of blue wardrobe, ties this kids room together.

A serene and traditional pooja room design that brings peace and spirituality to the home.
