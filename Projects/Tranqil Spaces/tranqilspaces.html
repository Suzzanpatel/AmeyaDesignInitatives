Tranquil Spaces

Style:

Contemporary Modern

Area:

1050 sq.ft

Project Overview

This residence is a celebration of thoughtful design and meticulous detailing, where warmth and elegance harmonize effortlessly. The seamless blend of wood, teal, and white tones, complemented by carefully curated partitions, false ceilings, and bespoke furniture, creates an open yet distinctly defined spatial experience. Each partition brings individuality to the zones while maintaining a cohesive flow, making the home both inviting and comfortably intimate.

Design Challenges & Solutions

One of the primary challenges was managing the structural beams that traverse the house. Through iterative design revisions and on-site adaptations, we successfully integrated these elements into the overall aesthetic, turning potential obstacles into defining features that enhance the spatial rhythm.

Kitchen & Dining

Anchoring the kitchen’s calm, neutral palette is a vibrant yellow glass-top breakfast table that adds an unexpected pop of colour. The beige membrane cabinetry and thoughtfully incorporated wooden accents make the kitchen not only visually appealing but highly functional and welcoming—a true heart of the home.

Master Bedroom

Designed to exude understated royalty and cozy comfort, the master bedroom is a study in warmth and sophistication. The curved, wave-inspired false ceiling softly envelops the room, while the warm lighting highlights the luxurious wooden finishes. Rich upholstery, elegant back paneling, and subtle gold T-patti accents imbue the space with regal charm. Every carefully considered detail contributes to a cohesive and inviting sanctuary.

Guest & Children’s Rooms

A striking balance between colour and neutrality defines the guest bedroom, where brick red, light wood laminates, and jute-textured finishes blend with minimalist design. The curved mirror and ambient mood lighting give the room subtle character and warmth—demonstrating how colour can be incorporated gracefully without overwhelming the space.
