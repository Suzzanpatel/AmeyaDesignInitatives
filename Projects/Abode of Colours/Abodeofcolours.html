An Abode of Colours

Where Every Room Tells a Story

Scope

Interior Designing

Location

Residential

A space that is personal to all individuals, this pooja unit, is compact yet very serene. It gives a peaceful aura and is so welcoming.

The use of fluted glass on the side enhances the space and that along with the lotus MDF cut door, makes the unit unit look delicate and elegant. A blend of the materials- ply, laminate, MDF and glass- this unit comes together so beautifully.

To find a suitable area in a house for a pooja unit is very important. Keeping in mind the Vaastu and space constraints in apartments, we made sure that the pooja unit continues to give positive vibes to the home and the residents of the house.

A blend of modern, bohemian and vibrancy - this foyer space living room gives us all the feels and is super welcoming. With the beautiful artwork of Lord Ganesha in the foyer, followed by the openness of the living room, this space is calming yet happening!

Adorned by the right amount of warmth and light, the artwork in the foyer gives such a serene entrance to the house. Continuing to the living room, the brick wall (material texture paint) along with the curtain and the grey colour scheme adds a lot to the theme of the room.

The different moods that the lighting of this room gives is something that makes us very happy! With a mix of warm white and yellow lights, there is a light for all moods in this vast living room.

The design ideas have ranged so vastly from our initial designing to our final product. We started with blue, transitioned to yellow and at the end came up with a final colour of bold red.

In this house, every room has its own identity by the usage of colours, and the kitchen definitely leaves a mark with it's red. The colour that not only resonates with the residents of this house, but also gives the kitchen its own individuality.

The bold red color scheme creates a vibrant and energetic atmosphere in the kitchen, making it a standout space in the home.

This master bedroom aims to give a cool, breezy, free look to it. With the blue colour being a highlighting wall- the dandelions in white and the other furniture binding the entire look together.

The carefully chosen furniture and decor elements create a harmonious blend of style and comfort in the master bedroom.

The feature wall adds depth and character to the bedroom space, creating a stunning focal point.

Thoughtfully designed storage solutions that blend seamlessly with the room's aesthetic while maximizing space utilization.

Every corner of the home is designed with attention to detail, creating a cohesive flow throughout the space.

The architectural details and interior elements work together to create a sophisticated and welcoming atmosphere.

Custom design elements that reflect the homeowner's personality while maintaining the overall design aesthetic.

Carefully selected accent pieces that add character and charm to the living spaces.

The attention to detail in every design element creates a luxurious and comfortable living environment.

High-quality finishes and materials that ensure both beauty and durability in every space.

A final showcase of the project that demonstrates the perfect harmony of colors, textures, and design elements throughout the home.

A combination of the colours blue & pink with the curtain the theme of Frozen, it's a room that gives a space of it's own to this energetic girl.

A lot of colours are difficult to play with, but taking these pastel shades was definitely a good choice. The room comes together very well when looked at it in a whole, with the storage being a highlight of the room.
