Abode of Colours

Style:

Colour Pop

Area:

800 sq.ft

Project Overview

This residence is a thoughtful balance between functionality and emotional comfort — a home where every room has a unique identity while still belonging to a cohesive design language. From a serene pooja unit to a vibrant kitchen, the space transitions seamlessly across moods, materials, and colours. Each zone is designed with purpose — integrating Vaastu principles, maximizing utility in a compact urban layout, and introducing personalized details that reflect the personality of its inhabitants.

Pooja Unit

A home’s energy begins at the entrance, and this one sets a serene and welcoming tone. The foyer, enhanced by a graceful Lord Ganesha artwork, opens into a calming yet lively living area. Designed with an elegant balance of materials and light, the space blends modern charm with spiritual grounding. The compact pooja unit, a personal and sacred space, is thoughtfully integrated into the design. Featuring a lotus-cut MDF door and fluted glass panel, the unit feels both delicate and intentional. A balanced palette of ply, laminate, MDF, and glass brings cohesion and elegance. Mindfully placed in accordance with Vaastu and spatial constraints, this unit invites a continuous flow of positivity into the home.

Living Room

Warm, textured, and mood-driven, the living space is anchored by a brick-textured feature wall and grey-toned furnishings. Individual pieces—carefully chosen for their form and feel—come together in visual harmony. Layered lighting, with a mix of warm and cool tones, creates dynamic moods throughout the day and night. The layout celebrates openness while maintaining a cozy character.

Kitchen

Initially conceptualized in cooler tones, the kitchen evolved through a vibrant journey—ending with a bold red that now defines its identity. This colour not only resonates with the homeowners but infuses the space with energy and individuality. Functional and spirited, the kitchen stands out while still blending seamlessly with the rest of the home.

Kids Room

Imaginative and full of personality, the kids’ bedroom draws inspiration from the "Frozen" theme, combining pastel blue and pink hues with soft curtains and functional design. Playful yet balanced, the room provides a joyful retreat for a spirited young girl, complete with MDF panelling and thoughtful lighting details.

Master BedRoom

Designed to evoke calm and openness, the master bedroom pairs a breezy blue feature wall with soft white dandelion motifs, creating a restful yet expressive space. The furniture, lighting, and materials work in harmony, reinforcing a design language that is cool, clean, and airy. Pastel tones and subtle contrasts lend the room an understated elegance that reflects the homeowners’ lifestyle and preferences.
