AMEYA HOUSE

Construction & Design

Scope

Construction, Designing

Location

Sevasi, Vadodara

A very prominent site in the upcoming Sevasi area, of the city of Vadodara the site is surrounded by roads on four sides and canal on the west. Being four sided road made it very ideal to design the project as per Vaastu principles, with absolutely no compromises.

Modern office space design with clean lines and contemporary architecture.

Professional entrance design with company branding.

Efficient storage space design with industrial aesthetics.

This is an office space and go-down for AMEYA PERFOMATT an industrial quantity chemical company. The space is divided at 4150 sqft of office space and 2300 sqft of go-down space and the client wanted the project to get completed in a jiffy we went for pile foundation and pre engineered building. Glass wool was word to insulate the roof also the use of gypsum false ceiling to cut down the heat. For the walls use of aerocon fly ash bricks made work very easy and efficient, it cuts down on the labour cost and doesn't require curing either.
