Kitchen & Dining

Renovation Project

Scope

Interior Designing

Size

300 sqft

A modern kitchen design featuring sleek grey cabinetry, pendant lighting, and a functional layout optimized for cooking and storage.

Kitchen seen in its different moods due to the play of light and colours. The use of earthy tones makes the space pleasant and sophisticated. The hanging lights give an indication of a nest, an element of nature, and the use of line joints in the beam area gives the user a connection with texture and a vibrant feel of space.

The vibrant red colour is used sparingly to bring zing into the kitchen. It gives life and vibrancy to the open and white kitchen. Storage at its best, with proper use of space and easy accessibility to different materials which are used to prepare food. This makes life easier and faster for the user.

Memories are forever and giving them a priority space in your home makes it a memorable experience of the space. The dining wall highlighted with family memories down the ages. The use of metallic stucco texture makes it even more catchy with grey and white.

The wooden false ceiling with hanging lights divide the kitchen and dining highly effectively and is aesthetically pleasing.
