False Ceiling Design

When we design a space, a lot of things come to play. The ceiling is a very important part of a room- needing to blend it with the beams, giving proper light points, adding to the design and being minimal. The use of false ceiling is to have a proper distribution of light in a room, and to make the surface smooth for painting.
